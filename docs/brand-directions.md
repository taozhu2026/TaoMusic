# TaoMusic v0.2 Brand Identity MVP

This document captures three lightweight brand directions explored for TaoMusic v0.2. The goal is not a full brand package. The goal is to establish one credible, memorable, screenshot-friendly identity direction that fits the current product.

## A. Constellation / Signal

**Rationale**

- Connects directly to the product idea of contextual signals becoming a small music constellation.
- Feels poetic and slightly cerebral.
- Works well in editorial layouts and product diagrams.

**Assets**

- [`public/brand/constellation-signal-logo.svg`](../public/brand/constellation-signal-logo.svg)
- [`public/brand/constellation-signal-mark.svg`](../public/brand/constellation-signal-mark.svg)

**Wordmark pairing**

- Best with a restrained serif wordmark and fine mono subtitle.
- Slightly more “concept product” than “music object”.

## B. Vinyl / Orbit / Resonance

**Rationale**

- Most directly music-related without feeling retro or obvious.
- The orbital arcs suggest context, drift, resonance, and reroll movement.
- The simplified circular mark stays strong at favicon size.
- Pairs naturally with the current warm editorial UI.

**Assets**

- [`public/brand/vinyl-orbit-logo.svg`](../public/brand/vinyl-orbit-logo.svg)
- [`public/brand/vinyl-orbit-mark.svg`](../public/brand/vinyl-orbit-mark.svg)

**Wordmark pairing**

- Best with the existing refined serif “TaoMusic” wordmark.
- Gives the product a small-object identity rather than a startup dashboard feel.

## C. Refined TM Monogram

**Rationale**

- Most compact and brand-system friendly.
- Feels literary and composed.
- Useful if TaoMusic later grows into a broader suite.

**Assets**

- [`public/brand/tm-monogram-logo.svg`](../public/brand/tm-monogram-logo.svg)
- [`public/brand/tm-monogram-mark.svg`](../public/brand/tm-monogram-mark.svg)

**Wordmark pairing**

- Strong in small UI surfaces and social avatars.
- Slightly weaker on the immediate “music” cue compared with the orbit direction.

## Recommendation

The recommended direction for v0.2 is **B. Vinyl / Orbit / Resonance**.

Why:

- strongest music association
- easiest favicon and app icon translation
- fits the current warm, minimal, editorial UI
- supports future share cards and screenshots better than the more abstract constellation option

## Integrated Direction

v0.2 should integrate only the Vinyl / Orbit / Resonance direction into:

- header lockup
- favicon / app icon
- share-card branding
- metadata icon references
